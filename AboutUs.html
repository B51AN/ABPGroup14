What exactly is ABP about?

ABP is a Business program developed by the founders Solomon, Price, Alexandra and Brian to help change the environment. It is based in Ireland, and it was created in 2023. So how exactly did this start? We always see rubbish all over the place and got sick and tired of it, so we decided to do something about it and created ABP. ABP is targeted for people who are very motivated and are willing to help us make a change to this world. The end goal of ABP is to create a world where there is less rubbish and littering, and hopefully we can keep the environment and other ecosystems clean, so if you are ready to help us achieve that, click that sign-up button and come on this journey with to change the world.
